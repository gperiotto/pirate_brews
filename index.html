All hands ahoy matey we be selling brews since 1688, Pirate Brews is one of London's finest themed pubs, with over 100 bottled beers from around the world, and of course our world famous Pirate Brew Rum drink. The food menu is packed with flavours that are sure to shiver ye timbers.

Walk the plank and dive into a uniquely themed pub, revered for its eclectic range of pirate themed ales and its quality pirate themed pub food, which are served, as they should be, with a generous measure of famous Landlubber’s hospitality. Rediscover your inner pirate with pubs themed around the famous Golden Age of Piracy.

Fresh, seasonal ingredients are used on the Pirate Brews on menu to create traditional tastes of the Seas. To start, there are Slow-Cooked Beef, Amber Ale & Mushroom Pie that is Encased in shortcrust pastry with a puff pastry lid, served with mashed potato, seasonal greens and honey-roasted carrots. Fresh seafood mains include Sustainably Line-Caught Cod Fillets, Wholetail Scampi, there are vegetarian delicacies like Glazed Shallot Tart Tatin that is Topped with honeyed goat’s cheese pearls and Braeburn apple salad. The beer selection at Pirate Brews is famous, with everything from pale ales to porters served on tap. The 100+ bottled selection is regularly updated, and for wine lovers there are over 50 New and Old World vintages served.

Pirate Brews, founded by Privateer Handsome Jack, Faced with the black spot from some of his ruthless rival pirate Jamaal Beard Jones, Jack decided the pirates life ain't for he as he had to much to live for. Thus he changed his name to Sir Handsome Jack and sold his ship. Using all the riches he had claimed from his pirating days Jack and his crew opened the first Pirate Brews in hopes to find a more peaceful life and to give pirate folk alike a place they can call home. Unfortunately for Jack his pirating days caught up to him and a mere year later of opening the pub Jack was killed by a bounty hunter named Jango Beard The Fett. Luckily one of his crewmate Scallywag took up the challenge to carry on Jack's legacy.
